3 The model

Origination begins with demand.

Most land comes to market and then looks for a buyer. We invert that. We start from demand that already exists, define the parcel it requires, and then find it.

01

Model the buy box

We build a buyer's acquisition criteria from years of recorded transactions — location, format, traffic, rents, and price discipline — until the target parcel is defined by demand rather than by whatever happens to be available.

02

Tie up the land

We secure the matching parcel directly with the owner, underwriting access, utility, entitlement, and exit before any capital is committed.

03

Deliver the matched parcel

The underwritten parcel is delivered to the matched buyer whose demand defined it.

5 About

Jeremy Rolf, Founder.

Jeremy Rolf underwrites land like a principal. He works across the pieces most people see separately — commercial brokerage, IRS Enrolled Agent tax work, commercial lending, and ground-up development. The range lets him find land where tenant demand, capital structure, and execution risk align before the market prices it. He starts with demand — traffic, access, utility, exit value — then works backward to the parcel. As Development Manager at Stock Five Development, he helped move a stalled 8.85-acre site in Lincoln, California through bankruptcy, foreclosure, distressed debt, entitlement, and traffic-signal complexity. The position was taken near $3.5 million against a $5.43 million payoff and a $5.88 million appraisal — advancing toward a 7-Eleven-anchored development with additional pads and hotel use. He sees what has to be true before the market does.
