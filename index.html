Principal

Mayfair acts as a principal — a developer that acquires for its own pipeline. In plain terms: a real buyer, with real capital, that commits and closes cleanly. Not a broker, not a middleman, and nothing contingent on finding someone else first.

3 The model

From demand to development.

Most land comes to market and then looks for a buyer. We invert that — starting from demand that already exists, we define the parcel it requires and go find it, so what reaches you already fits.

01

Model the buy box

We start with demand, not inventory. Working from your criteria and deep market data — location, format, traffic, rents, price discipline — we define the parcel that fits, then go find it, instead of showing you whatever's listed.

02

Tie up the land

We secure the matching parcel directly with the owner, underwriting access, utility, entitlement, and exit before any capital is committed.

03

Develop, deliver, or partner

We advance the site and develop it, deliver it development-ready, or partner with the builder it fits — matched to the demand that defined it.

5 About

Jeremy Rolf, Founder.

Jeremy Rolf sees demand before the market does.

He builds proprietary geospatial intelligence — applications and data systems that model where development pressure is actually building, parcel by parcel — so Mayfair pursues the land demand requires, not whatever happens to be for sale. Most developers react to listings. Jeremy works backward from the demand itself: traffic, rooftops, access, rents, exit value.

His range is unusual. He reads a deal's tax and capital structure the way most developers read a site plan, and he's taken stalled, distressed land through the hardest parts of the work — bankruptcy, foreclosure, complex debt, entitlement — and turned a stuck asset into a credit-anchored development.

He sees what has to be true before the market does. Off the clock, he's a girl dad to three daughters and firmly third-in-command at home — behind his wife and Ziggy, a 25-pound Maine Coon who's convinced the whole place is his.
